GlowGuard

Automatic

Automatic adjusts dimming and blue light filter based on time of day:

  • Day (6am–6pm) — light dim, no filter
  • Evening (6pm–10pm) — medium dim, soft filter
  • Night (10pm–6am) — stronger dim, warm filter

Bright pages get extra dimming automatically.

min max
Alt+Shift+G Toggle on / off
Alt+Shift+Up Increase dim
Alt+Shift+Down Decrease dim
Alt+Shift+W Cycle blue light filter
